Candied Bacon

Candied Bacon Directions

This is a quick and simple recipe for candied bacon that will change how you enjoy your bacon!


Things you will need

  • Your favorite cut of bacon
  • Brown sugar
  • Foil
  • Oven pan

Steps to delciousness

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees
  2. Line oven pan with foil and place bacon on the foil
  3. Grab your brown sugar and sprinkle it even on each strip
  4. When oven finishes preheating, place pan in ovev
  5. Cook bacon for about 3 minutes on each side
  6. Take bacon out and let cool for a bit and enjoy!
