Hot Pot Directions

This dish is known as Hot Pot. It is seen to be as a social meal where family and friends communal pot of broth where you can cook some flavorful ingredients to share. The cooking process takes only but a few seconds!


Things you will need

  • Hot Pot dish kit (Comes with pot and heating eye)
  • Your favorite broth (Local oriental markets have specific broths for this)
  • Water
  • Thinly sliced meats and seafood
  • Vegetables
  • Your appetite!

Steps to delciousness

  1. Setup your eye and pot on the table
  2. Add water till it is about a little over halfway full
  3. Turn on eye and wait till work starts to boil
  4. Add broth gently stir
  5. Start tossing in your ingredients and enjoy!

To make your meal more tasty, create a dipping sauce!
