Deviled Eggs

Deviled Eggs Directions

This recipe can be for a quick snack or for a party gathering


Things you will need

  • Eggs
  • Sweet pickles
  • Your favorite brand of mayo
  • Smoked paprika
  • A pot
  • Water

Steps to delciousness

  1. Place your into the pot
  2. Fill pot till the water is just above the top of the eggs
  3. Place the pot on the stove and turn it to "Hi"
  4. Let the eggs boil till there is just little water left in the pot
  5. Take pot of stove and put eggs on ice
  6. Deshell eggs
  7. Cut eggs vertically and scoop out the yolk careflly and place into a bowl
  8. Grab your mayo, smoked paprika, and sweet pickles
  9. Add each ingredient to taste and texture. Use the juice of the sweet pickles (you can cut up and add the sweet pickles if you'd like)
  10. Once it's to your taste, add them back into the egg whites
  11. Sprinkle smoked paprika on top
  12. Place in the fridge till chilled and enjoy!
